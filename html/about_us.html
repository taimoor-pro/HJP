About Us

Hamdard University was founded by Shaheed Hakim Muhammad Said in 1991. Soon after establishment of this largest Private Sector University in Pakistan, Faculty of Pharmacy was introduced by him in 1997. In honor of his services this Journal is dedicated to Shaheed Hakim Mohammad Said which is published by Faculty of Pharmacy and to continue what he believed in; dignity, honesty, knowledge, faith and spirit of services. What makes us unique is our dedication and following of footsteps of our beloved founder who believed in developing new horizons in knowledge, growth and promotion of good health.

Our peer-reviewed bi-annual journal is focused on real, integrated solution of health issues and for a better perspective of improvements in health care system, drug delivery system and drug design. We believe in quality research publication with convenience. This Journal is open access and we will provide forum to showcase the bold innovative ideas and progress in field of Pharmacy. We have excellent advisory board and constructive reviewers who not only critically evaluates but facilitate researchers. We shall cover all the areas of Pharmacy and allied healthcare sciences for the cutting edge ideas that will be free to access at economical hassle free manner to academia and researchers.
